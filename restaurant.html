About Panoor Restaurant

Panoor Restaurant is a well-known South Indian/ Malabar cuisine chain, established in 1983, famous across the UAE, Qatar, and KSA for its authentic, budget-friendly dishes like Chatti Choru, Biryani, and exceptional tea/chai, serving classic non-veg Indian, Keralite, and some Chinese options in a simple, family-friendly setting with great hospitality.
